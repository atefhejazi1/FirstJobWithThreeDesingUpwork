Strategy CRITERIA

Alston Capital Partners targets investments principally in the environmental sector. We will consider opportunities in other sectors in which Alston's principals and network have deep industry knowledge or have prior investment experience.

Alston believes that the target sectors provide opportunities to invest in companies that can benefit from our ability to assist in identifying and choosing growth initiatives, enhancing operating platforms, professionalizing financial and managerial reporting systems, and executing strategic mergers and acquisitions.

investment CRITERIA

Transaction Types

Control or co-control buyouts, recapitalizations, divestitures, growth equity, turnarounds and minority investments.

Target Sectors

Environmental

Target Company Location

Headquartered in the United States and Canada

Target Company Size

Companies generating between $5 million and $50 million of EBITDA (minimum EBITDA does not apply to turnaround opportunities). We also have access to large pools of capital allowing us to make much larger investments
